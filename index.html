Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2025

1. Overview

Facebook Poster ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our social media posting application.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect or Store

We do not collect, store, or retain any of the following:

  • Facebook access tokens
  • User account information
  • Posted content or messages
  • Facebook page data
  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

3. How Our Application Works

Our application operates as a direct interface between your browser and Facebook's servers:

  • You provide your own Facebook access token
  • All communication happens directly between your browser and Facebook
  • No data passes through or is stored on our servers
  • Tokens are only held in your browser's memory during active use

4. Data Security

Since we do not store any user data:

  • There is no user data at risk on our servers
  • All connections use HTTPS encryption
  • You maintain full control over your Facebook permissions

5. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with Facebook's Graph API. Please review Facebook's Privacy Policy for information about how Facebook handles your data.

6. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over:

  • Your Facebook access tokens and permissions
  • What content you choose to post
  • Which Facebook pages you grant access to
  • Revoking access to our application at any time through Facebook

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our application or website.

10. Compliance

This application complies with Facebook Platform Policies and applicable privacy regulations.
